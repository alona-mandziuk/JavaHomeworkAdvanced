The Asgardian Loki encounters the Other, the leader of an extraterrestrial race known as the Chitauri.
In exchange for retrieving the Tesseract, a powerful energy source of unknown potential the Other promises Loki an army with which he can subjugate Earth.
Nick Fury, director of the espionage agency SHIELD, arrives at a remote research facility, where physicist Dr Erik Selvig is leading a team experimenting on the Tesseract.
